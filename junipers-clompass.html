Juniper's Clompass

Re-enchanting learning through analog-digital hybridity

How might we re-enchant learning through analog-digital hybridity? Juniper's Clompass turns a 1970s analog clock into a digital compass and storytelling device for children. By fusing creative literacies with technical literacy, the Clompass develops new methods of learning that inspire curiosity through making, narrative, and exploration rather than traditional instruction.
