Art

Creative Practice

A collection of mixed media artworks exploring texture, materiality, and color through embroidery, textile arts, and experimental creative practices. Below is a snapshot of recent exhibitions, collaborative projects, publications, and community-centered teaching that shape this practice.

Selected Exhibitions & Presentations

  • Women’s Biennial, 2020 — Mount Saint Mary’s University, Los Angeles
  • The University of Memphis
  • Phoenix Zine Fest, 2020
  • Bay Area Queer Zine Fest, 2018
  • Long Beach Zine Fest, 2018
  • NY Latin Flair Fest, 2017

Selected Projects

  • Rebrew: A Pocket Apothecary — Awarded Best Student Design, SF Design Week, 2024
  • Acorn House — Interactive children’s exhibit exploring Indigenous storytelling and play
  • UniiFriend AI Agent — AI learning companion for children supporting emotional engagement
  • Show Me Your Colors — SEL tool using art, ritual, and color for emotional literacy

Publications

  • Decolonizing Parenting Zine (Co-editor, 2018–2020)
  • Self-published zines (2017–present) on Afro-Indigenous cosmology, grief, and survival
  • Children’s Book (in progress) on multiplication awareness for children of color

Teaching & Community Engagement

  • Art Educator, Albany Unified School District, 2023–2024
  • Social Justice Fellow, UC Berkeley, 2024–2025

Awards & Funding

  • Best Student Design, SF Design Week, 2024
  • Arcus Social Justice Fellowship, UC Berkeley, 2024–2024

Workshops

  • Abode Press Speculative Fiction Fellow, Summer 2025
